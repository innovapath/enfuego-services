Summary
The Legal Assistant will support and provide a range of legal administrative duties. The qualified candidate should be highly organized, detail-oriented and possess the ability to effectively work independently but also work well in a team environment. In addition this position requires the ability to maintain confidentiality. Exceptional verbal and written communication skills are necessary as well as interpersonal skills and the ability to work with and manage deadlines.
Essential Duties And Responsibilities
· Schedule meetings (Board meetings, pre-Board meetings, committee meetings, etc.)
· Reschedule meetings as necessary
· Reserve conference rooms for Board and Committee meetings
· Sending out items by UPS
· Copying
· Scanning
· Distributing documents on behalf of Legal for execution and return
· Tracking the return of fully executed documents
· Submitting invoices for payment
· Assist paralegal in compiling Board materials
· Assist in delivering hard copies of documents to Executive Officers for their review in preparation of the Board/Committee meetings
· Knowledge of Word and Excel.
· Performs other duties as assigned
