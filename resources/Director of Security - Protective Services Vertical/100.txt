Job description : 
The Director of Security will be responsible for the implementation and management of the corporate security strategy centered on a HITRUST framework.
Job Scope
Providing leadership and direction for the organization’s HITRUST focused security strategy. The Director of Security will build and sustain a Cyber Risk Framework that integrates Governance and Risk controls, through Information Technology (IT) and Operational. The Director of Security will be responsible for leading the quarterly security review and project managing all assigned new projects or remediation identified and approved by the information and security team. This position will also participate in change control ensuring a security focus is part of the change control process. The role will also work in collaboration with the Chief compliance officer and Director of compliance to ensure a Company-wide alignment between Security and Compliance.
Major Responsibilities
· Oversee the execution and maintenance of a HITRUST
certification
· Oversee an organization-wide governance processes to ensure
compliance with HITRUST Establish and manage processes for monitoring cyber security
strategies, policies, compliance controls, and programs to meet the company’s
business needs.
· Identify Information Security needs and risks, and establish
operational plans that align with the organization’s vision, mission and
objectives, and support long-term Information Security growth and
sustainability.
· Develop standard security operating procedures for
organization.
· URAC: work with compliance and accreditation team to review
and update policies and procedures. Implementation of changes and auditing of
policies for compliance.
· Respond to security questionnaires (physical and IT).
· Ensure HITRUST &
HIPAA compliance through IT security operations.
Qualifications
· 5+ years of related experience preferably within the
pharmaceutical/healthcare industry.
· Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science or related field.
· Security accreditations
· Program and project management experience.
· Experience in cyber risk reporting and establishment of key
risk indicators and key performance indicators.
· Experience in some or all information security technologies,
firewall, intrusion detection, assessment tools, encryption, certificate
authority, web, and application development.
· Expert knowledge of Microsoft Products.
· Capable of recommending vendor products, services, and
solutions to impact cost reductions and resource maximization.
· Knowledge of HIPAA compliance a plus.
· Experience with IT Enterprise Architecture and Governance
Risk and Compliance (GRC).
