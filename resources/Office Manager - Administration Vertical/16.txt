Tenth Avenue Holdings,LLC

Job title :Office Manager 
Job location : New York, New York
Employer name : Tenth Avenue Holdings,LLC
Job description : 
We are looking for an Office Manager to organize and coordinate administration duties and office procedures. Your primary role will be to create and maintain our work environment, ensuring high levels of organizational effectiveness and communication. We view this as an ideal opportunity for a motivated self-starter to add value to the team across a wide array of business areas – from general office administration to IT management to human resources and beyond. 
Office Manager responsibilities include coordinating meetings and appointments, managing office equipment, technology and supplies, supporting visitors to the office and providing general administrative support to our employees. Previous experience as an Office Manager or Office Administrator would be a definite plus. A successful Office Manager should also have experience with a variety of business software and be able to accurately handle a variety of administrative duties. Ultimately, the Office Manager should be able to ensure the smooth running of the office and help improve day-to-day operation of the business. 
Responsibilities 
· Schedule meetings and appointments for all members of the team, including travel arrangements and meetings hosted here in the main office 
· Maintain the office condition and arrange necessary repairs / upgrades; manage and regularly order office supplies 
· Coordinate with IT provider to ensure all office equipment and technology – from conference room AV equipment to employees’ work cell phones – is functional and up to date 
· Partner with HR to update and maintain office policies as necessary; assist in the onboarding process for new hires 
· Organize office operations and procedures 
· Assist partners and Controller with payment of office invoices; assist Controller with management of office G&A budget 
· Provide general support to visitors 
· Plan in-house or off-site activities, like parties, celebrations and conferences 
Requirements 
· Proven experience as an Office Manager or Administrative Assistant 
· Knowledge of office administrator responsibilities, systems and procedures 
· Proficiency in Microsoft Office (Outlook, Word and Excel in particular) 
· Hands on experience with office machines (e.g. fax machines and printers) 
· Excellent time management skills and ability to multi-task and prioritize work 
· Attention to detail and problem-solving skills 
· Excellent communication skills 
· Strong organizational and planning skills 
· High School degree required, Bachelor’s degree preferred
