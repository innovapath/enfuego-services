If you wish to apply, simply click the ‘Apply’ button and fill out the form or email your resume to: foodresumes@fepsearchgroup.com (no PDFs please) Job Description (partial List Of Responsibilities) ============================================== Key Duties Include Our client, a leading food and beverage company, is currently seeking an ambitious and gifted Chief Operating Officer to help lead their team. This is a unique opportunity for a highly motivated leader with a go-getter attitude and a drive to succeed.

 Develop mechanisms that work in support of the overall vision, business strategy and goals

 Communicate goals and objectives to employees and monitor all business operations to ensure congruence to and achievement of stated objectives

 Oversee the financial resources to ensure the execution of an effective system of internal controls

 Act with integrity, honesty and knowledge to promote the core values of the organization

 Represent the team professionally through effective communication, cooperation and relationships with all business partners

 Monitor all business operations and develop standard operating protocols to strengthen the organization

 Promote a cohesive team environment among staff and managers that encourages cooperation, creativity and excellence

 Work collaboratively with company finance to ensure uniform preparation of budgets and consistent financial reporting

 Manage business activity and outcomes to ensure alignment to and progress towards the overall business plan

 Delegate responsibilities to empower those under you to continue to grow and contribute to the properties

 Advise advisory group on strategies, development and key corporate planning issues

 Make recommendations on major business decisions

 Lead the negotiations on any newly proposed business relations, striving to achieve outcomes for the best interest of the company

 Work collaboratively with the founder to develop a long-term business plan

 Communicate the business plan and vision to both managers and staff

 Provide leadership to create a culture and work environment based upon respect

 Identify opportunities for staff to learn, grow and develop their abilities

 Identify and mentor your successor

 Attend key industry shows and maintain close relationships with key sales representatives with industry knowledge Qualifications And Desired Skills ======================

 A university degree is required. Although all candidates are welcome and encouraged to apply, preference will be given to those with degrees in a relevant field

 Appropriate full-time work experience is mandatory (no students or fresh graduates please)

 Recent experience in the food and beverage industry is strongly preferred

 Preference will be given to candidates with recent experience in operations management For the right leader, this is a wonderfully rewarding role with tremendous growth prospects.
