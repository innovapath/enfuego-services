St. Jude Medical is now Abbott.
We are seeking an experienced, high caliber Quality Engineer to take part in our Quality Rotational Development Program. This position is responsible for developing and maintaining quality engineering methodologies and providing quality engineering support within new product development, manufacturing, or system/services support.
Our Quality Rotational Development Program is designed to help you start a rewarding career in the Quality organization within the medical device industry. Our program provides broad exposure to High Potential Engineers to different aspects of Quality, diverse cross-functional partners, multiple Abbott product lines, and different Abbott Cardiovascular and Neuromodulation sites. The program’s focus is to accelerate skill development in Quality through structured assignments and professional mentoring. Our program offers a unique opportunity to take advantage of fast-track learning experiences specific to the high demand functional areas of Quality. During this 36-month program, associates will rotate through several twelve-month assignments in functional areas of Quality at one of our Abbott Cardiovascular and Neuromodulation sites. Employees act on their strong desire to make a difference, partner with others and put ideas into action. Employees are engaged by a work culture that is team-oriented, fast paced and progressive.
Each employee can make a difference at Abbott and has the power, either individually or as a team, to influence the success of the company. We are team-oriented, fast-paced and progressive. We value people with great ideas who partner with others both internally and externally to take action and accomplish goals.
