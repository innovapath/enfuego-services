Job description :
DJO Global, Inc. is a leading global provider of high-quality, orthopedic devices, with a broad range of products used for rehabilitation, pain management and physical therapy. We also develop, manufacture and distribute a broad range of surgical reconstructive implant products. We are currently seeking an outstanding Manufacturing Lead for 2nd shift at our Corporate Headquarters in Vista, CA.
Job Description: In charge of the assigned production processes or cells. Team up with Shift Supervisor to organize and recommend strategies in ensuring that the product is processed successfully, in accordance with all process and product specifications. Manufacturing Lead will direct the activities of the team members, scheduling their work to meet daily, weekly and monthly goals. This challenging position places high premiums on effective teamwork, safety compliance, clear communication, initiative, intelligent judgment, achieving and maintaining production efficiency and, most importantly, an unwavering dedication to the quality of the product produced.
Responsibilities
· Ensure safe work environment.
· Implement Lean manufacturing practices and principles.
· Lead the production team. Mentor and motivate team members.
· Direct the activities of the team members and provide the team specific directions in carrying out assigned tasks.
· Monitor team members in the course of their work and ensure that the team complies with the standards of operations.
· Assume responsibility for quality output: Check/verify finished products to ensure quality; and that quality standards are met before they are moved to the next process.
· Keep records of workers performance.
· Create work schedule for production team and ensure that they strictly adhere to it; monitor attendance to ensure regularity and promptness of workers to duty.
· Act as a communication hub between team members and leadership team to create healthy communication.
· Ensure availability of material supplies throughout the shift.
· Assist in evaluating manufacturing equipment for safety and functionality.
· Assist in performing hands-on production and assembly functions whenever required.

About Djo Global
DJO Global is a leading global developer, manufacturer and distributor of high-quality medical devices that provide solutions for musculoskeletal health, vascular health and pain management. Our products address the continuum of patient care from injury prevention to rehabilitation after surgery, injury or from degenerative disease, enabling people to regain or maintain their natural motion. Our products are used by orthopedic specialists, spine surgeons, primary care physicians, pain management specialists, physical therapists, podiatrists, chiropractors, athletic trainers and other healthcare professionals. In addition, many of our medical devices and related accessories are used by athletes and patients for injury prevention and at-home physical therapy treatment. Product lines include rigid and soft orthopedic bracing, hot and cold therapy, bone growth stimulators, vascular therapy systems and compression garments, therapeutic shoes and inserts, electrical stimulators used for pain management and physical therapy products. Our surgical division offers a comprehensive suite of reconstructive joint products for the hip, knee and shoulder. Our products are marketed under a portfolio of brands including Aircast®, Chattanooga, CMF™, Compex®, DonJoy®, ProCare®, Exos™, Dr. Comfort®, DonJoy Performance® and DJO® Surgical.
