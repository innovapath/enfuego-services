Job title :Office Manager 
Job location : Chicago, IL, US 
Employer name : Kellogg Company
Job description : 

Grow with us as you will be responsible for administrative management of Kellogg’s downtown Chicago office operations. As the Office Manager, you will provide support services to team members residing in the Chicago office and liaising back to Kellogg headquarters in Michigan. As an Administrative professional, you will perform specific tasks or assignments of a unique, sensitive or confidential nature that will require independent judgment and discretion. While you are at it, you’ll be building your network and expertise in an environment of professional development and teamwork. Together, we’ll build a stronger future for our company and your career.
WHAT WILL I BE DOING? 
In your role as an Office Manager at Kellogg, you will be part of our success by:
· Be the face of the office to internal and external employees and visitors
· Responsible for overall day to day operational functions of the office
· Manage the conference and meeting rooms, including managing the ordering and inventory of supplies
· Coordinate with Kellogg Information Technology team to ensure success of the office operations and enable team in the Chicago location
· Conduct special projects, manages calendars, prioritizes meetings, screen calls and visitors, and handle requests for information
· Coordinate travel arrangements verifying accuracy and completeness of travel documents and plans
· Support the team with bookkeeping and financial management of overhead budget
· Compose and/or prepare responses and correspondence requiring research and exercise of judgement and discretion
WHAT DO I NEED TO DEMONSTRATE? 
Required
As well as a hunger to learn, the ideal candidate will have excellent self-motivation, organization and collaborate skills. To be considered for this position you must be able to meet the following requirements:
· Associate’s Degree
· Significant experience in office administration and senior level administrative support
· Substantial experience with Microsoft Office Suite
· Demonstrated ability to understand and support basic overhead budget
Preferred
· Bachelor’s Degree
· Excellent project management skills
