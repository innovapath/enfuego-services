Job description
Constangy, Brooks, Smith & Prophete LLP seeks a full-time Legal Assistant to join its busy team in Century City, California. The Legal Assistant will support attorneys with labor and employment litigation matters. Firm seeks candidates with several years of solid experience in litigation procedures and processes, and strong word processing and document editing skills.
Must be highly proficient with filing and e-filing in State and Federal courts, litigation calendaring and deadlines, preparing legal documents and correspondence, travel arrangements, scheduling, billing time entries, and other administrative and support duties as needed. Qualified candidates will have strong skills with Microsoft Office (Word, Outlook and Excel), document management systems, litigation calendaring software, and general business technology.
This is a team-based atmosphere, with a competitive compensation package plus benefits. The position is available immediately and is an excellent opportunity for a talented and motivated legal assistant.
If you are interested and believe you meet these requirements, please send your resume to lgardner@constangy.com for immediate and confidential consideration.
