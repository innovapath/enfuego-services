Job Summary
Summary: Expert in information security architecture. Develops and maintains a forward looking Nuance information security reference architecture leveraging the Nuance Information Security Controls Framework. Applies a wealth of industry knowledge and an encyclopedic knowledge of the security vendor space to develop leading edge and forward looking solutions to security defenses. Assists Corporate Director of GRC in maintaining the control framework. Directs Mobile Hosting information security governance risk and compliance (GRC) duties. Designs and maintain a holistic approach to governance risk and compliance by applying and integrating industry best practices into the top level business processes in Mobile.
Uses thought leadership to design and architect around traditional barriers to security control adoption. Works alongside development, operations, and IT leaders to understand existing solutions and provide expert advice in control implementation. Advises and assists Mobile Hosting resources as required.
Exercises wide latitude in determining objectives and approaches to critical assignments. Develops and drives remediation for critical issues by leading process redesign where necessary. Creates formal networks with key decision makers and serves as external spokesperson for the organization on matters related to Mobile Hosting information security. Maintains overall Mobile Hosting information security customer facing documents.
