Job Brief: 
We are looking for an office manager to organize and coordinate administration duties and office procedures. Your role is to create and maintain a pleasant and professional work environment, ensuring high levels of organizational effectiveness, communication, and care. 
Office manager responsibilities include scheduling meetings and appointments, making office supplies arrangements, greeting visitors, and providing general administrative support to our employees. Previous experience will be an advantage. A successful office manager should also have experience with a variety of office software (email tools, spreadsheets, and databases) and can accurately handle multiple administrative duties. 
Ultimately, the office manager should be able to proactively ensure the smooth running of the office and help to improve company procedures and day-to-day operation.
