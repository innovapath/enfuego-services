Manufacturing Group Leader – Kennesaw
At Philips, we strive to make the world healthier and more sustainable through innovation. Our goal is to improve the lives of 3 billion people a year by 2025. We will be the best place to work for people who share our passion. Together we will deliver superior value for our customers and shareholders.
Your Responsibilities
As Manufacturing Group Leader, you will be responsible for coordinating and monitoring the work of assembly workers as directed by work center management on the first shift- 6:45 am to 3:15pm (overtime is required).
Additionally you will be responsible for
· training, teaching and coaching workmanship quality on all work that is completed
· leading projects
· setting up the line for the day’s work
· giving a brief overview of the day’s work to the 1st shift staff at the start of the shift
· responding to and resolving multiple issues daily
· actively participating in one or more improvement teams
Our offer
You will work in the Kennesaw facility for Philips Respironics, a global leader in sleep and respiratory markets, we are passionate about providing solutions that lead to healthier patients, practices, and businesses.
We are looking for
· strong leadership and interpersonal skills
· a high level of initiative and sense of responsibility and ability to work independently
· commitment towards completing company goals
· a high level of attention to detail
· experience ensuring compliance to policies, procedures and work instructions
· ability identify and resolve potential issues to workflow, procedures and documentation issues
· experience adhering to good safety and housekeeping practices
· should be action oriented, organized, and timely in decision making
· H.S. or GED required; college degree a plus
· Minimum 3 years directly related experience is preferred
· 2+ years Assembler experience required
· Experience with Word, Excel and Outlook, proficiency with standard office computers and equipment
· Ability to frequently move or transport product of ten pounds and sometimes up to 70 pounds
· May also need to access above the level of a standard 54 inch file cabinet
· Ability to work shift hours are 6:45 am to 3:15 pm. Group Leader must be in earlier for line set up and stay after shift ends. Some Saturdays will be required.
