Company Description

At Red Hat, we connect an innovative community of customers, partners, and contributors to deliver an open source stack of trusted, high-performing solutions. We offer cloud, Linux, middleware, storage, and virtualization technologies, together with award-winning global customer support, consulting, and implementation services. Red Hat is a rapidly growing company supporting more than 90% of Fortune 500 companies.

Job Summary
The Red Hat Facilities Operations team is looking for an Office Manager to join us in Mountain View, CA. In this roll, you will manage the day-to-day operations of the Mountain View office, while supporting the Global Workplace Solutions (GWS) Facilities Operations team to ensure the success of daily activities and functions, including reception, ticketing services, PO support, vendor support, and various Red Hat events. We'll need you to have excellent communication, organization, and customer service skills to help take this location to the next level while also participating in global projects and initiatives with innovative ideas and enthusiasm. Come prepared to work in a fast-paced, cross-functional environment and contribute to Red Hat's operational success.

Primary Job Responsibilities
· Perform daily ticketing management, triage, and updating
· Track invoices and create PO (receiving, budget tracking, etc)
· Oversee office communication and office mailing list administration (general communication such as lost keys, new program or process rollout, facility notifications)
· Manage associate resources, e.g. building access requests, airport directions, menus, parking passes, or wireless access cards
· Provide reception support, including security (visitor check-in, visitor log management, temporary and contractor badge administration) and answering/transferring phone calls within defined service levels
· Manage office supplies, including ordering, taking inventory, and stocking shared local office supplies, copy station supplies, and breakroom supplies
· Order, take inventory, and stock building supplies, conference rooms management and support, and condition assessment (status of paint, carpet, storage, overall clutter, etc.)
· Assist with new hire onboarding and offboarding (new associate building tours, desk setup, etc.)
· On-site storage management (file drawers and closets)
· Evaluate service and cost to select vendors for the local operation
· Coordinate the arrival and direction for on-site vendors and validate deliveries
· Manage personal credit card and petty cash
· Coordinate activities to support cross-functional office events, meetings, and parties
· Participate on shared group committees, both local and national
· Support various local and national projects and programs
Required Skills
· Experienced in delivering exceptional customer service
· Solid written and verbal communication skills; ability to maintain a professional business appearance at all times
· 5+ years of related experience
· Bachelor's degree or equivalent; relevant certifications will also be considered
· Office management experience in a mid-large sized facility
· Solid organizational skills, with an attention to detail
· Proficient working knowledge of workplace tools including documents, spreadsheets, and presentations
· Great research, discovery, and critical thinking skills
· Comfortable in a highly collaborative environment with the ability to work well with cross-functional teams
· Ability to prioritize, problem solve, manage time effectively and work with minimal supervision
· Positive and enthusiastic attitude with the ability to adapt to changing project demands
