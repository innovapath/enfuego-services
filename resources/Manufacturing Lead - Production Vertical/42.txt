Job title : Manufacturing Lead
Job location : Memphis, TN, US

Employer name :
Job description :
Lead the inventory management efforts between the local sales unit, local business unit and manufacturing facilities for the assigned products of responsibility. Generate unconstrained demand plan, measure, report, and drive improvement of demand planning accuracy. Run the monthly inventory management process, track and monitor monthly inventory improvement initiatives, maintain inventory check book covering the inventory of the value chain of the local unit. Ensure material availability through PFEP (Plan for Every Part) and execution of purchase orders between the pull system and manufacturing facilities.
Tasks
Responsibilities:
Demand Planning
· Creates and maintains rolling 18-24 months unconstrained demand forecasts for assigned products. Assembles and analyses all data pertinent to creating an unconstrained demand plan. Obtains Sales’ sign-off on the demand plan for input to the Integrated Business Planning process.
· Works with Product Management team to coordinate new product launches, platform changes etc. and incorporate them into the demand plan.
· Monitors finished goods Stock Keeping Units (SKU) levels, recommends SKU rationalization initiatives and feeds them into the inventory check book.
· Reviews and improves forecast accuracy both at the aggregate and the individual product level by identifying key root causes for forecast errors, and implements initiatives to improve future forecast accuracy.
Inventory Analysis
· Performs standard and ad hoc detailed analyses for inventory (raw material, work In progress and finished goods). Identifies key root causes for excess inventory, and implements improvement initiatives.
· Runs weekly/monthly inventory management process, maintaining inventory check book with inventory initiatives to forecast inventory on rolling 12 months basis.
· Compiles and sends weekly/monthly report to local business leaders on inventory status vis-a-vis the targets.
Material Planning
· Conducts piece part stock out analysis, identifies root causes and assigns follow-up actions to appropriate value chain function.
· Selects and optimizes finished goods inventory levels to maintain at targeted service levels. Analyzes excess inventory, identifies root cause and assigns follow-up action to appropriate value chain function.
· Conducts monthly supply chain management review covering manufacturing delivery performance, material availability, manufacturing lead times and quality. Identifies and performs appropriate actions when manufacturing facilities do not meet defined performance standards.
· Releases purchase orders (pulls), expedites parts and manages quick ship requests. Holds daily meetings with Production Planners, Receiving/Inspection and Customer Care to communicate and expedite materials issues. Conducts Purchase Order Date Management Review.
· Ensures accuracy of ERP with regard to sourcing rules, supplier lead times, planning time fence, fixed day supply, minimum order quantity etc.
· Leads part classification into ABCD or ABC/XYZ in order to optimize purchasing quantities, order patterns (e.g. just in time) and best methods of materials management.
