Job title : Legal Assistant

Job description :
Greenberg Traurig, a global law firm, currently has an excellent full-time employment opportunity for a Government Affairs Legal Assistant in our Tallahassee office. We offer competitive compensation and an excellent benefits package.
Duties & Responsibilities
Responsibilities include but not limited to scheduling appointments, meetings and travel; attorney billing and client contact; expense reimbursements; lobbyist registration and quarterly compensation reporting. Must be available to work overtime as needed.
Qualifications & Prior Experience
Position requirements include: a Bachelor’s Degree or equivalent education and work experience. Candidate will have previous experience in Governmental Affairs and/or legislative affairs. Legal experience is a plus. Proficiency with Windows-based software and Microsoft Word, Excel, Power Point and Outlook; proficient in electronic case filing with the various courts systems; excellent word processing skills; the ability to multi-task and work with little or no supervision.
