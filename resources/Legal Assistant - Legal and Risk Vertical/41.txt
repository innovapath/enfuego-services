Job title :   Legal Assistant

Job location :  Boston, Massachusetts

Job description :
Lawson & Weitzen, LLP seeks a full-time litigation legal assistant to join our team to Support our healthcare defense partner. Ideal candidates should be self-starters with the ability to work in a team environment as well as work independently. Duties include:
· managing, maintaining and organizing documents;
· maintaining electronic calendar, case files, electronic billing and database;
· client financials
· scheduling depositions, service of process, document production;
· Discovery and trial preparation;
· Review monthly draft bills; attend to general office tasks including preparing;
· Powerpoint presentations and spreadsheets, obtaining public records and timely delivery of documents to courts and opposing counsel.
Required Skills & Abilities:
· Computer proficiency; specifically in Microsoft Outlook and Word;
· self-motivated, resourceful and the ability to prioritize, multi-task and be highly attentive to detail;
· Exceptional written and verbal communication skills;
· excellent customer service skills and the ability to communicate with clients, court agencies and vendors in a courteous and professional manner;
· strong organizational and interpersonal skills;
· Preference given to candidates with Paralegal Certificates.
Lawson & Weitzen, LLP offers a full benefits package along with a generous paid time off policy. Conveniently located on the Silver Line route; onsite parking is also available. Salary commensurate with experience.
