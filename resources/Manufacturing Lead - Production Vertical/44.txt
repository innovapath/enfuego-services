The Syracuse Biologics Manufacturing operation produces clinical, commercial and launch biologics drug substance at its 5000L scale manufacturing facility (mammalian cell process) in Syracuse, NY.
The Manufacturing Technology Scientist/Engineer will support Manufacturing Operations (upstream processing, inoculum, cell banking, facilities and materials management) on a daily basis, as well as lead or contribute to strategic projects.
· Identify and lead opportunities with matrix and departmental teams focused on continuous improvement in the areas of productivity, robustness, quality, quality risk management, business process, and safety.
· Perform regular and frequent on-floor observation to support and improve manufacturing.
· Lead Quality Investigation teams to determine root cause
· Ensure investigations correctly assess product impact and identify appropriate CAPA.
· Originate, review and evaluate change controls.
· Support PPQ studies and BLA filings including on-floor support, data verification and data analysis.
· Support and lead technical transfer of new molecules, perform facility fit and lead implementation of product/process transfers between manufacturing facilities.
· Collect process data, perform statistical analysis and identify opportunities for improvement of process performance.
· Author protocols and final technical reports for requested initiatives/ studies.
· Review and approve manufacturing batch records (MBR), standard operating procedures (SOP), and other cGMP documentation, as required.
· Follow cGMP requirements.
· Participate in network-wide alignment and optimization of Manufacturing Technology business processes.
