Program Manager

L3

USA-Utah-Salt Lake City

Job description

Summary  Responsible for managing government program(s) and directing all phases of the program(s) from inception through completion  Responsibilities: 

· Responsible for the cost, schedule and technical performance of company programs or subsystems of major programs

· Acts as customer contact for program activities, leading technical program review sessions with customer to discuss requirements, cost, schedule, and performance

· Serves as company interface to US Navy customers, providing reach-back to other areas of company operations (e.g., engineering, manufacturing, business development activities).

· Monitors and controls the repair activities both in-house and at customer location(s)

· Creates both internal and customer reports providing status of current repair activities, inventory management, and utilization of equipment

· Leads program planning and budgeting activities.

· Prepare estimates at complete (EAC) for production, installation, and repair activities using standard cost and schedule reports.

· Participates in the negotiation of contract and contract changes. Coordinates the preparation of proposals, business plans, proposal work statements and specifications, operating budgets and financial terms/conditions of contract.

· Establishes milestones and monitors adherence to master plans and schedules, identifies program problems and obtains solutions, such as allocation of resources or changing contractual or technical specifications

· Directs the work of employees assigned to the program from technical, manufacturing and administrative areas

Qualifications Qualifications:

· Technical degree and a background in an Engineering discipline and/or project/program management is preferred. Experience in lieu of a B.S. degree will be considered.

· Ability to obtain a Security Clearance is required

· Experience is working with NAVSUP and DLA in supporting Navy systems is desired.

· Knowledge of DoD inventory management and supply chain operations desired

· Experience developing proposals conforming to FAR, including basis of estimates and pricing desired.

· Experience with the use, test, or design of wideband communication systems desired.

· Proven ability to successfully manage cross-functional teams in matrix environment to develop and deliver technologically advanced systems is desired.

· Familiarity with SAP or other MRP and Supply Chain Management software desired.

· Ability to travel (up to 20%) and experience in meeting with customers in business development environment is desired.

Experience in lieu of a degree will be considered. This position may be filled at a higher grade based on the successful applicant's skills, education and experience.
