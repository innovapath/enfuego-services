Job description :
Overview: As the manufacturer of Dart® and Solo® brand products, Dart Container is an industry leading single-use food and beverage packaging company. With 15,000 employees and 40+ locations worldwide, Dart offers more than 4,000 products used every day in restaurants, hospitals, schools and homes around the globe.
Benefits: Dart Container full-time employees enjoy great benefits, including medical, dental, vision, life insurance, 401(k) with company matching contributions, paid vacation, paid holidays, short-term disability, tuition assistance, product discounts and more.
The Legal Assistant provides administrative support to Dart in-house Attorneys, including but not limited to, record maintenance, filing, drafting correspondence and reports, meeting preparation, maintaining inventory of office supplies, and answering phones.
Day-to-Day Role
· Retrieve, scan and upload corporate records to appropriate databases
· Update department documents and resources such as organizational charts, stock certificates and tax/banking records
· Draft correspondence at the direction of in-house Attorneys
· Review and update corporate resolutions with direction from in-house Attorneys
· Be a primary conduit for communication amongst the in-house Attorneys
· Perform daily office routines such as greeting and assisting visitors, answering phones, ordering and maintaining office supplies, and sending and receiving shipments
· Maintain confidentiality, anticipate needs and utilize discretion and tact in daily work
· Maintain records according to established processes. Develop and implement new record maintenance processes.
· Ensure department office equipment is functioning. Contact appropriate personnel to troubleshoot or perform maintenance.
Core Skills and Qualifications:

· High school diploma or equivalent
· Two (2) years of experience providing administrative support to a professional team
· Strong attention to detail and accuracy
· Organizational skills including the ability to prioritize workload to meet deadlines
· A highly motivated self-starter with excellent interpersonal skills
· Ability to maintain composure and adapt in a dynamic, fast-paced, customer-focused work environment characterized by rapid change, minimal lead times, and multiple competing priorities
· Ability to maintain confidentiality
· Proficient in Microsoft Word, Excel and PowerPoint
· Experience researching credible and updated information on the Internet for project work
· Ability to effectively manage and execute multiple tasks, projects and priorities
· Excellent verbal and written skills necessary in order to communicate intricate job-related processes
· Ability to work additional work hours on short notice required
Preferred Skills And Qualifications
· Paralegal degree
· Associate's degree with an emphasis in business
· Legal or paralegal administrative support experience
· Experience using SharePoint
· One (1) year of experience maintaining a professional filing system
· One (1) year of experience ordering and maintaining office supply inventory
