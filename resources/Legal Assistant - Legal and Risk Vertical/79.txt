Providing various support activities to attorneys in a legal setting including but not
limited to determining eligibility for legal services in accordance with laws.
Legal support workers perform highly specialized work requiring knowledge of technical terminology and procedures and protocols. They prepare correspondence and legal papers such as summonses, complaints, motions, responses and subpoenas under the supervision of an attorney or a paralegal. They also review legal journals and assist with legal research by verifying quotes and citations in legal briefs.
