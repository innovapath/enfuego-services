Job title :Office Manager

Job description:
Growing downtown Chicago law firm seeks office manager to manage the day-to-day operations of the firm. Our new office manager must be organized as well as have strong accounting, human resource, and management skills. 
Main responsibilities include: 
· Preparing clients invoices 
· Preparing financial report 
· Maintaining the office policy manual
· Developing and maintaining office systems and procedures
· Supervising administrative staff and general office coordination. 
Candidates must have at least two years’ experience as an office manager in a law or other professional service firm with bookkeeping experience. Bachelor’s degree in accounting/business highly preferred.
