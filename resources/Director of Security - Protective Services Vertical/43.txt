International Flavors & Fragrances, Inc. is currently seeking a Director of IT Security to join its IT organization based in Union Beach, New Jersey. Reporting to the Vice President of Infrastructure Services, this is the senior IT leadership role that oversees the people, process, and tools of information and cyber security capabilities along with IFF’s global, 90 site voice, data and video network. The Director of IT Security performs multiple core functions for the enterprise. The first is to agree policies, develop standards and guidelines and align information security priorities with the CIO who reports to the CEO and CISO who reports to the General Counsel. The Director oversees the design, build, and deployment of a next-generation multi-pronged security and network defense capability that includes operating a Security Operations Center (SOC) located in our captive Chennai offices. The Director is accountable for establishing an enterprise security stance through policy, architecture and training processes. Secondary tasks will include the selection of appropriate security solutions, and oversight of any vulnerability audits and assessments.
The Director of IT Security is expected to interface with peers in Information Technology as well as with the leaders of the business units to both share the corporate security vision with those individuals and to solicit their involvement in achieving higher levels of enterprise security through information sharing and co-operation.
Major Accountabilities
Strategy & Planning - 30%
· Ensure the creation and maintenance of the enterprise’s security architecture design.Manage selected outsource vendors and partners who help the Director deliver the services suite.
· Aid the CISO with maintaining a cadence around the security awareness training program.
· Present at the monthly Security Steering Committee along with preparing regular updates for the corporate Board of Directors
· Ensure the creation and maintenance of the enterprise’s security documents (policies, standards, guidelines and procedures).
· Prepare financial forecasts and budgets for security operations
· Prepare senior-level technical reports for executive management.
· Support the continuous planning, development and implementation of a secure global infrastructure designed to protect against all matter of threat to firm resources and data.
· Connect legal, regulatory and local organizational requirements with security goals
Program and Project Management - 30%
· Ensure the creation of a robust governance process around deploying our funded security tool set that includes enhancements to:
· SIEM
· IDS/IPS
· Multi-Factor Authentication and updated anti-malware protections
· Upgraded global WIFI security and access controls
· Enhanced Network segregation
· Data Loss Prevention solutions
Operational Management - 30%
· Ensure the confidentiality, integrity and availability of the data residing on or transmitted to/from/through enterprise workstations, servers and other systems and in databases and other data repositories.
· Ensure the enforcement of enterprise security documents.
· Supervise all investigations into problematic activity and provide on-going communication with senior management.
· Supervise the design and execution of vulnerability assessments, penetration tests and security audits.
· Contribute to regular security awareness training for all employees to ensure consistently high levels of compliance with enterprise security documents.
· Engage in ongoing communications with peers in Information Technology as well as the various business groups to ensure enterprise wide understanding of security goals, to solicit feedback and to foster cooperation.

· Manage relationships with third party providers of services to the firm. Responsibilities include negotiation of contract language and evaluation of third party risk related to privacy and security practices.

· Provide supervision of staff managers, engineers, vendors, auditors, and other persons tasked with maintaining information security.
Acquisition & Deployment - 10%
· Maintain up-to-date knowledge of the IT security industry including awareness of new or revised security solutions, improved security processes and the development of new attacks and threat vectors.
