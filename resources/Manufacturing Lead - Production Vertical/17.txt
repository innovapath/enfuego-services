Job description :
Boeing Military Aircraft has a current opening for Strategic Execution Leader, Level M Manager.
Working with and supporting the VP-Boeing Military Aircraft (BMA) Manufacturing and St. Louis site leader, this leader in this position will provide and lead cross-functional process management teams to address multiple, high-impact business processes to drive 21st century processes and tools. The selected individual will also lead teams that drive process integration in support of BDS Plant Centric Operation (PCO) initiatives to include macro level assessments of PCO initiative impacts on Operations’ Estimates at Completion (EACs) across the enterprise. This leader will work across the business to analyze, document and integrate multiple business requirements from multiple process owners. Additionally, this manager will oversee the recommendations for the process, data, and applications related improvements within Operations and Mfg. to include benchmarking and adoption of process management best practices. This manager will also have the responsibility for leading BMA Manufacturing strategy and execution in BMA. The position is focused on implementation and execution of key business objectives across BMA Operations.
Specific Responsibilities Include
The successful candidate should have the ability to analyze and assess complex issues, data, and situations, providing guidance and leadership on solutions. Additionally, the leader must be a self-starter with the ability to lead and manage multiple projects and have demonstrated leadership strengths in written and verbal communication, sound judgment and decision-making ability with an attention to detail. The successful candidate should also have demonstrated professional demeanor and initiative with a thorough knowledge of Boeing processes. This leader will need to develop and maintain relationships and partnerships with customers, stakeholders, peers, partners and direct reports. Additionally, the chosen candidate will provide oversight and approval of technical approaches, products and processes. As a leader, it will be necessary to manage, develop and motivate employees.
· Define strategy/plan for overhead at BMA core/site level and manage execution of overhead budget

· Develop long-term Operations strategy in concert with programs and division
· Translate strategy to action in support of funding
· Support prioritization/budgeting processes and help drive execution of projects
