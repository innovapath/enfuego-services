Ad Astra information Systems

Job title :Office Manager 
Job location : Overland Park, Kansas
Employer name : Jackie Shaver
Job description: 
Help us achieve our goal of making Ad Astra a best places to work! We are looking for a passionate and effective Office Manager to help us with our day to day operations. This position will be responsible for supporting our internal operations and team members. We are looking for an energetic professional who doesn’t mind wearing multiple hats. This position will include advancement opportunities for the right candidate. 
Responsibilities:
· Improve employee engagement by proactively supporting all Astra staff, showing an eagerness to help and becoming the go to person for all departments.
· Be the face of Ad Astra by greeting customers and answering the phone
· Maintain office services by organizing office operations and procedures; controlling correspondence; designing filing systems and supporting all clerical functions as necessary.
· Manage, maintain, and stock office supplies as well as create and review supply requisitions.
· Maintain office efficiency by recommending and investigating office systems, layouts, and equipment procurement.
· Work closely with the administration and operations teams to complete filing, billing, accounts receivable, accounts payable, and scheduling tasks as necessary
· Assist with the recruitment, orientation, and training of employees.
· Work closely with sales to coordinate trade shows, regional summits and marketing activities as necessary.
· Coordinate all company meetings and events.
· Maintains professional and technical knowledge by attending educational workshops; reviewing professional publications; establishing personal networks; participating in professional societies.
Requirements: 
· Associate Degree 
· Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite 
· Advanced computer skills 
· Supply Management experience preferred 
· Project Management experience 
· Excellent verbal and communication skills 
· Excellent time and project management skills 
· Comfort with fast paced and changing environment
