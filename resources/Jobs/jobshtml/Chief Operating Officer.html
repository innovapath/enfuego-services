ABOUT THE OPPORTUNITY. The Chief Operating Officer (COO) provides the leadership, management and vision necessary to support a rapidly expanding system of charter schools. The COO creates the proper operational controls, administrative and reporting procedures, and people systems to effectively grow the organization and to ensure financial strength and operating efficiency. The COO will have ultimate responsibility for the following departments across multiple schools: human resources, facilities, enrollment/compliance, information technology, and food services.

· Collaborate with the executive team to develop overarching business goals and strategies for operational growth both in state and out of state (AZ, NV, TX, and others).

· Spearhead the development, communication and implementation of specific business processes, physical infrastructure, and personnel designed to accommodate school enrollment growth and operational effectiveness.

· Lead, develop and motivate department directors for successful day-to-day execution of school business operations. Motivate and lead a high performance management team. Attract, recruit and retain required members of the management team not currently in place. Provide mentoring and training.

· Provide day-to-day leadership and management that mirrors the adopted mission and core values of the company.

· Create and drive departmental adherence to annual budgets. Continually seek ways to optimize operational processes and drive revenues.

· Work with outside legal counsel and internal HR department to resolve legal disputes (e.g., dissatisfied parents of students, other school-related grievances).

· Identify business growth opportunities and potential new revenue streams.

· Represent the company with clients, investors, governmental entities, and other outside parties.

· Foster a success-oriented, accountable environment within the company.
