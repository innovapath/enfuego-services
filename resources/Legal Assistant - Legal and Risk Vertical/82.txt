Job description :
The Cincinnati office of Squire Patton Boggs (US) LLP is hiring an Executive Legal Secretary to join our team. This position primarily supports the Financial Services and Litigation practice groups. Responsibilities include preparing PowerPoint presentations, managing busy calendars, arranging travel, preparing expense reports, revising and proofreading legal documents; telephone support; arranging conferences, and client meetings, among many other secretarial duties. The ideal candidate will have advanced executive level secretarial skills, some legal experience, superior judgment and the ability to work well under tight deadlines.
We require an Associate's degree or equivalent from a two-year college or technical school or at least three years executive level secretarial experience, preferably in a law firm or other appropriate comparable experience Must possess strong written and interpersonal skills and experience in Microsoft Office.
We offer excellent benefits, competitive compensation, and the opportunity to work in a professional, collaborative work environment.
