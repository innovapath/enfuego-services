Job description: 
The United Nations Children's Fund (UNICEF) works in 190 countries and territories to put children first. UNICEF has helped save more children’s lives than any other humanitarian organization, by providing health care and immunizations, clean water and sanitation, nutrition, education, emergency relief and more. The U.S. Fund for UNICEF (USF) supports UNICEF's work through fundraising, advocacy and education in the United States. Together, we are working toward the day when no children die from preventable causes and every child has a safe and healthy childhood. For more information, visit www.unicefusa.org .
Major Gifts Intern – Los Angeles (LA Regional Office)
USF seeks an intern to join our Southern California Region team interested in learning fundraising, donor relations, and donor stewardship. The Southern California Region internship is located in the Los Angeles regional office.
The Internship Will Provide An Opportunity To
· Develop knowledge of fundraising activities and donor relations
· Website Management and list management
· Event Planning
· Good writing, editing, communication and interpersonal skills
· Strong critical thinking and analytical skills
The intern will gain skills that will prepare them for effective leadership in public service and organization of fundraising and community outreach programs. The intern will also learn about UNICEF’s programs and domestic initiatives
Key Responsibilities/Projects
· Participate in organizing donor cultivation events
· Perform prospect research of individuals, private family foundations and corporations within the Southern California market alongside the Major Gifts fundraising team
· Develop skills in drafting various fundraising, stewardship, and solicitation communications
· Collaborate with the Los Angeles Global Citizenship Fellow on special community outreach opportunities
· Participate in special event planning to include support of the quarterly Speaker’s Series Luncheon and private donor cultivation programs
· Work on other projects as they arise
Qualifications
· Must be a current student enrolled in a College or University degree program; Focus on International Development a plus
· Knowledge of Microsoft Word; Excel a plus
· Strong interest in fundraising and stewardship preferred
· Excellent interpersonal skills and attention to detail
· Strong written, editing, and oral communication skills
· Willingness to learn and be creative
· Must demonstrate USF Core Values: Trust, Respect, Accountability, Innovation, Teamwork, and Service
Time Frame: Summer 2017
Days per Week: Flexible
This is an unpaid internship.
