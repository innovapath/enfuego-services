Sr. Director of Security Operations
USA - Georgia - Alpharetta - 30005
The Sr. Director of Security Advisement Team is responsible for leading strategy, lifecycle, and maintenance of the existing technical security controls that protect Equifax. This role will also be responsible for making sure that all M&A’s are brought into the Equifax standard technical security control set by ensuring best practices are implemented uniformly across the Equifax network. Lastly, this role will be a player-coach and participate in advisements and be responsible leading and growing a small team of engineers that will work closely with internal Business Units and the Cyber Intelligence team to ensure Equifax security controls are up to date and capable of mitigates current and likely threats to Equifax networks.
Position Requirements
· Experience with integrating/implementing security controls for new M&A’s and managing dynamic requirments while still being effective
· Prior experience managing security personnel of all levels
· Define, gather, and report on applicable metrics regarding the delivery of security monitoring within the Equifax environment
· Experience and knowledge with Cloud Security and experience working with Cloud Security vendors (Amazon, Microsoft and Google)
· Expert knowledge of network and security protocols and principles such as: TCP/IP, defense in depth, granularity of privilege, etc.
· CISSP (Certified Information Systems Security Professional) certification required. CISA (Certified Information Systems Auditor) certification desired.
Position Attributes
· B.S. Computer Science/Engineering or similar technical degree program
· 10-15 years previous technical experience with networking and/or software development background
· Expert knowledge regarding Network Security Mitigation practices and consulting
· A pragmatic outlook that understands security “problems” as a balance of security and business requirements
· Experience with multiple OS platforms, to include Linux, Unix, Windows and AIX
· Experience in monitoring and managing network and host-based intrusion detection/prevention, data loss prevention, malware detection/prevention, vulnerability scanning solutions, DDOS protection, Security Event/Information Management, host-based integrity checking, end-point security, AV, Proxies, Database Monitoring, etc.
Core Competencies
· Results Focused: Focuses on the critical few objectives that add the most value and consistently delivers results that meet or exceed expectations.
· Imports and Exports Good Ideas: Relentlessly seeks, shares and adopts ideas and best practices in and outside the Company and embraces change introduced by others.
· Teamwork: Lead team and ensure theymeet all team deadlines and responsibilities, listens to others and values opinions, helps team leader to meet goals, freely provides needed information, proactively supports other team members, welcomes newcomers and promotes a team atmosphere, builds relationships across the organization.
· Problem Solving/Analysis: Breaks down problems into smaller components, understands underlying issues, can simplify and process complex issues, understands the difference between critical details and unimportant facts.
