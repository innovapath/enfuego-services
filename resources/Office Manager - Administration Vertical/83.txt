KPMG is currently seeking a Seasonal Portfolio Management Office Manager, to join our Global Service Center Organization.

Responsibilities
· Manager sub-functions of Advisory Innovation’s Portfolio Management Office utilizing the portfolio and project management tool Clarizen
· Understand the correlation between governance requirements and the portfolio management and reporting approach; manage changes in portfolio management and reporting resulting from evolving requirements from leadership and through collaboration with other operational teams such as finance, quality and risk
· Understand reporting requirements of different innovation partners and stakeholders to generate multi-level portfolio and project reports; keep pace with changing stakeholder needs and modify reporting accordingly
· Support leadership decision making by generating useful and meaningful executive level reporting and manage a defined cycle of reporting to streamline the information capture process
· Supervise the work of the PMO’s onshore and offshore technical resources; coach and guide project managers on the established governance model and reporting requirements; enable project teams with the right tools and guidance so they can best manage and streamline their projects and reporting; act as a primary point of contact for governance and reporting questions from project teams
· Consult with other strategic internal groups to align on portfolio governance and reporting and extend reporting and platform support to these internal groups as needed

Qualifications
· Minimum of five years of recent relevant business or industry experience; minimum three years of Program/Project management or PMO experience
· Bachelor’s degree from an accredited college or university; Project Management professional qualification such as CAPM / PMP / PRINCE2 is preferred
· Knowledge of the KPMG Advisory Business and the services offered is preferred
· Experience working with virtual and geographically distributed teams
· Past experience with at least one project management tool such as Clarizen, MS Project or Primavera; proficient in Excel and PowerPoint
· Demonstrated advanced organizational, analytical and communication (written and oral) skills
