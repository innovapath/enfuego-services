Job title : Manufacturing Lead

Job description :
Position Description
Leads the Directorate`s Advanced Manufacturing initiative and associated program development opportunities. Participates in defining strategic directions in the initiative and contributes to long-range strategic planning. Serve as primary lead for the development and maintenance of broad funding base to support these programs and associated R&D research activities. This role is a key member of the Directorate leadership team working directly with Associate Lab Director and/or other senior managers across the divisions and other directorates.
Responsibilities Include, But Not Limited To
· Coordinate all laboratory activities related to the Advanced Manufacturing initiative and corresponding programs.
· Manage program communications and marketing efforts.
· Work with laboratory technical leads in developing program strategies and partnerships.
· Have primary responsibility for sponsor relationships.
· Work with other laboratory thrust leads to leverage programs and capabilities.
· Report to laboratory management on program status and program-development activities.
· Manage designated LDRD portfolio, including funding decisions and project oversight.
· Other duties as assigned
Position Requirements
Knowledge, Skills and Abilities
Comprehensive
· Skills in program development, direction, and execution.
· Skills in developing and maintaining effective relationships with a diverse set of programmatic sponsors, influencing future directions and pursuing funding opportunities.
· Knowledge of and recognition in one or more technical areas of Advanced Manufacturing and/or associated programs.
· Skill in decision-making, innovative problem solving, and adapting knowledge to complex situations.
· Excellent verbal and communication skills and the ability to work with all levels of internal and external contacts and to clearly express ideas and achieve desired goals.
Considerable
· Knowledge of Advanced Manufacturing initiatives and associated programs; some knowledge of all directorate technical areas is required.
· Knowledge of the availability and technical skills of personnel required for program development and execution.
· Knowledge of management concepts, methods, and techniques.
· Knowledge of the Laboratory`s organizational structure and funding sources.
· Knowledge of Laboratory and DOE policies and procedures.
· Interpersonal skills and the ability to function effectively either as a leader or team member
Other: Q security clearance or ability to obtain the same; SCI clearance is desirable. Experience working with DoE Office of Science and with DoD preferred.
