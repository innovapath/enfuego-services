Arconic
Job title : Manufacturing Lead
Job location : Minneapolis, Minnesota

Employer name : Emily Cicconi, CDR

Job description :
The customer service representative serves as liaison between customers, the S&OP department, Program Management and the Commercial group.
· Under minimum supervision, serves as the point of contact for customer inquiries and calls.
· Coordinates (and performs when applicable) Contract Review activities for all new orders
· Checks status on orders for customers, and expedites orders.
· Enters and processes requests for quotes and order confirmations.
· Provides weekly updates for weekly status reports.
· Tracks weekly/monthly delivery performance with customer by use of graphics and KPI’s
· Regular participation in conference calls with customers addressing high-priority issues.
· Assist in identifying appropriate resources required to meet program deadlines
· Assist in planning internal and external program communications, ensuring effective exchange of program management information and deliverables
· Coordinates on a day- to- day basis, contract and subcontract deliverable status as well as in house inter-departmental activities to ensure deliverables yield to schedule.
· Serve as a focal point for and liaison between departments on program status or program information
· Keep customers informed of program progress per contract and ensure customer is aware of any action items requiring attention.
· Coordinates and participates to Program Reviews with Customer.
· Attendance and participation at regular departmental meetings, program management calls, and other internal responsibilities.
· Travel may be required
Basic Qualifications :
· Bachelor’s degree from an accredited institution
· Minimum 2 years customer service experience
· This position is subject to the International Traffic in Arms Regulations (ITAR) which requires U.S. person status. ITAR defines U.S. person as an U.S. Citizen, U.S. Permanent Resident (i.e. 'Green Card Holder'), Political Asylee, or Refugee.
Preferred Qualifications :
· Ability to read and interpret documents such as safety rules, operating and maintenance instructions, and procedure manuals.
· Ability to write routine reports and correspondence.
· Ability to speak effectively before groups of customers or employees of organization.
· MS Office including Microsoft Word, Excel, Power Point, e-mail.
· CRM software like SalesForce
