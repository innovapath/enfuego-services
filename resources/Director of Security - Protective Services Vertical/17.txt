Job description :
We are currently hiring for a Director of Cyber Security Breach Detection. This position is located in Las Vegas, NV.
Position Overview
The primary responsibility of the Director US SOC Cyber Security is responsibility for the day to day operations of the US SOC, the equipment that supports the SOC and the training and operational readiness of the analysts that support SOC operations. The Senior Manager US SOC Cyber Security is also responsible for maintaining communications with other SOCs identified by the Executive Director of Global Cyber Operations, the property IT departments and property analysts to support the operations of the SOC and for cyber security response/support to events that affect the security of the LVSC networks.
All duties are to be performed in accordance with departmental and Las Vegas Sands Corp.’s policies, practices, and procedures.
Keywords: cyber security collection, analysis, intrusion response and reporting process/procedures, GSEC, SSCP, Security+,CEH, GCIH, GCIA, GCED, GSE, CISSP, SIEM, TCPDump, Wireshark, Nessus, Metasploit, nmap, EnCase Enterprise, ForensicTool Kit (FTK), network security appliances, identity and access management (IAM) systems, anti-malware solutions, automated policy compliance tools, desktop security tools.
