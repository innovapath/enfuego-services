Advanced Civil Technologies

Job title :Office Manager 
Job location : Santa Ana, California
Employer name : Advanced Civil Technologies
Job description : 
Advanced Civil Technologies, a growing company that is focused on providing transportation solutions to public and private clients, seeks a detail-oriented Office Manager. 
The person hired for this position will be an integral contributor to a growing company that is focused on providing transportation solutions to public and private clients. Applicant should be a self-starter that is ready to work in a small environment and committed to helping to further the growth of the company. 
Job Purpose: Maintains office services by organizing office operations and procedures; preparing payroll; controlling correspondence; designing filing systems; designing and documenting office policies and procedures; reviewing and approving supply requisitions; assigning and monitoring clerical functions, among other tasks. 
Job Duties: 
· Take responsibility for all office administrative operations. 
· Designs and implements office policies by establishing standards and procedures; measuring results against standards; making necessary adjustments. 
· Provides historical reference by defining procedures for retention, protection, retrieval, transfer, and disposal of records. 
· Keeps management informed by reviewing and analyzing special reports, summarizing information; identifying trends. 
· Facilitate annual bookkeeping activities, including tax documentation, audits, certification renewals, and insurance renewals, among others 
· Manage the internal accounting and time-tracking systems, including managing accounts payable and accounts receivable. 
· Assist with the development of monthly client invoices. 
· Maintains office staff by recruiting, orienting, and training employees. 
· Keep office-wide personnel records. 
· Provide assistance to employees on time-keeping policies and ensure timesheets are completed correctly. 
· Prepare necessary payroll data on a bi-weekly basis. 
· Facilitate HR activities for the firm, providing guidance to employees as necessary. 
· Complete benefit enrollments when appropriate. 
· Liaison for property management, building management, and support service vendors. 
Qualifications: 
Minimum education level of a Bachelor’s degree required. Degree in accounting, finance, or related field is a plus. The candidate shall possess a minimum of 6 to 10 years of experience. Previous experience working in a similar capacity in the A&E field is preferred. Previous experience with QuickBooks is required. Must have excellent written and oral communication skills, flexibility when under strict deadlines and a professional can-do attitude. Proficiency with Microsoft Excel, including specific experience with filtering data, pivot tables, and basic algebraic functions, is required.
