What you will be doing at Beachbody as an Executive Director Security Architect?
· Partner with Beachbody business units, including IT, to understand business processes, identify risks, and build secure solutions that enable the business to maintain a competitive advantage in the marketplace.
· Bring Hands-On Security Architecture and Engineering – The candidate must have extensive hands-on experience architecting and engineering secure enterprise solutions. It’s important for the Security Architect to be able to guide and enable the business.
· Perform Information Security Assessments – The Security Architect will perform security, vulnerability, and risk assessments of critical systems, infrastructure, and third parties. Recommendations and action plans will be developed based on these assessments. The Security Architect will guide the business by addressing these plans and recommendations.
· Oversee Threat and Vulnerability Management – The Security Architect will work closely with IT to identify risks, threats, vulnerabilities and provide remediation guidance.
· Manage Incident Response – The candidate must have incident response experience, particularly leading incident investigations.
