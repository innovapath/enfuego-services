The Operations Manager will oversee crude hauling operations for assigned areas and personnel providing crude trucking services to customers. The Operations Manager will analyze existing operating practices and implement best practices.
SUMMARY OF ESSENTIAL JOB FUNCTIONS
Maintain delivery calendars and manage communication to cross-functional business partners including Planning and Allocation, Finance, Merchants, E-commerce and IT
Responsible for budgeting, forecasting and accountable for profitability of the area
Maintain required pick up and deliveries for internal and external Customers
Develop staff and continuously evaluate processes for improvement opportunities
Uphold vendor compliance guidelines as well as DOT requirements
Optimize equipment and load utilization
Lead distribution team on best strategies in all functional areas, using past performance to goals as indicators
Establish and maintain effective working relationships with members of cross-functional teams (Buyers, Operations, Finance, E-commerce and IT)
Develop ongoing flow forecasts in partnership with internal buyers and external customers and understand open-to-buy impacts
Demonstrate ability to negotiate with and influence peers and members of cross-functional team.
Organize and prioritize workload to meet deadlines
Demonstrate logical analysis and problem solving skills; good listening, written and oral communication skills
Deliver strong and thorough presentations to all levels of management, clearly identifying business issues and recommending resolutions
Maintain cost-effective and efficient oversight of headquarters facilities
Act as a positive role model to peers and subordinates; motivate and lead department and cross-functional teams
Maintain strict compliance to DOT OSHA and all other regulatory agencies
