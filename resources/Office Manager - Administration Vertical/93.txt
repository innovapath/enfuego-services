Job description
Pop-Up Magazine Productions is offering a full-time Office Manager position, working with the teams that make The California Sunday Magazine and the live-events series Pop-Up Magazine. You’ll help with a wide range of administrative duties, from on-boarding new employees, to corralling contracts and invoices, mailing magazines, responding to customer service emails, and general office upkeep.

Candidates should be organized and confident managing multiple projects at a time. The Office Manager will receive a salary, health insurance, and equity in Pop-Up Magazine Productions, Inc. The position is based in San Francisco and reports to the president.
