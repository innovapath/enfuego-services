Our Client is the innovator of the world's largest and most accurate database of environmental and historical land use records and they are looking for a talented Director Of Infrastructure and Security to join their team. If you have the following skills, they are looking for you!
BASIC FUNCTION:
As the Director of Infrastructure and Security, you are responsible for providing technical direction, leadership and guidance relative to IT infrastructure, information and system security of EDR’s systems. This includes providing stewardship over hosting services and related technology, and the effective design and implementation of these solutions. This is accomplished through your understanding of our business strategy, overall IT infrastructure and security strategy, industry trends and available technologies. Additionally, you will lead all technical project build functions for EDR hosting solutions, computing systems, storage, networks, and telephony in our development, test, UAT and production environments.
